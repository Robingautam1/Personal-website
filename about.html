About

Who I work with

Mostly students, early-stage founders, and small brand owners. People who need something built but don't want to deal with an agency or figure out everything themselves.

I've worked on portfolio sites for freelancers, startup websites for first-time founders, and investor decks for people raising their first round. The common thread is that the people I work with are usually doing this for the first time and want it to go smoothly.

How I approach work

I try to keep things simple and clear. Before building anything, I share a rough plan so you know what to expect. During the work, I send updates regularly so nothing feels like a surprise. If something needs changing, we change it; not a big deal.

I don't overexplain or overwhelm with options. If you're not sure what you need, I'll help you figure that out too.

What I care about

  • Clarity The site or deck should make sense to the person looking at it, not just to you.
  • Structure Messy projects create stress. I prefer things organized from the start.
  • Timelines I stick to deadlines. If something comes up, I'll tell you early.
  • Communication I respond quickly and keep you in the loop. No ghosting.

If that sounds like how you'd want to work, feel free to reach out.
