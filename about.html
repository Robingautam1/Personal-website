About

A bit about me

I'm an MBA student at IIM Rohtak, with a background in leading student initiatives. I served as President of E-Cell at my previous university, where I led a 30-member team and engaged over 2,200 students through events and programs.

Before business school, I worked with student founders, early-stage startups, and NGOs. That's where I picked up the habit of structuring ideas clearly and building things that actually get used.

Who I work with

Students, founders, and growing teams. People who need something built but don't want to deal with an agency or figure out everything themselves.

I've worked on 20+ projects across portfolios, startup sites, and pitch decks. The common thread is that the people I work with are usually doing this for the first time and want it to go smoothly.

How I approach work

I try to keep things simple and clear. Before building anything, I share a rough plan so you know what to expect. During the work, I send updates regularly so nothing feels like a surprise. If something needs changing, we change it.

I use AI tools to speed up workflows and reduce busywork, so more time goes into the work itself.

What I care about

  • Clarity The site or deck should make sense to the person looking at it, not just to you.
  • Structure Messy projects create stress. I prefer things organized from the start.
  • Execution Ideas matter, but getting them done matters more.
  • Communication I respond quickly and keep you in the loop. No ghosting.

If that sounds like how you'd want to work, feel free to reach out.
